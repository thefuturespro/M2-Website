Philosophy

Markets do not move because of opinions, narratives, or indicators. They move because of pressure.

Trend is a byproduct. Direction is contextual. Entries are discretionary. M2 exposes expansion, absorption, and alignment across timeframes — without prediction, automation, or false certainty.
